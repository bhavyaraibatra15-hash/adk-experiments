# Bharat Antariksh AI

## Vision

Bharat Antariksh AI is an Agentic AI platform designed to make Indian space science accessible to everyone.

The system acts as an intelligent assistant for learning about ISRO missions, satellites, launch vehicles, astronauts, and future space programs.

---

## Problem Statement

Space information is often scattered across multiple sources and difficult for students to understand.

This project aims to simplify space education using AI agents.

---

## Features

### Mission Explorer

Search and understand ISRO missions.

Examples:

* Chandrayaan
* Mangalyaan
* Aditya-L1
* Gaganyaan

### Satellite Explorer

Learn about Indian satellites and their purpose.

### Space Education Assistant

Explains complex space concepts in simple language.

### Timeline Generator

Shows mission history and future roadmaps.

### AI Research Assistant

Provides summaries of space-related topics.
